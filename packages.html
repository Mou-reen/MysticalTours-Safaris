Our Travel Packages

Explore our carefully handpicked destinations, each designed to give you an unforgettable experience. On this page, you’ll find detailed itineraries that highlight the unique activities and attractions of each package, along with transparent inclusions and clear cost breakdowns. Whether you’re looking for relaxation, adventure, or cultural immersion, our packages provide all the essential details you need to make an informed choice and plan your journey with confidence.

Kenya

Nairobi National Park

Experience the only park in the world within a city! Witness the Big Five and breathtaking landscapes on this unforgettable safari.

Package Inclusions

The day's itinerary
  • Depart Nairobi early in the morning (around 6:00am) for Nairobi National Park.
  • Game drive through the park: Enter the park and enjoy a thrilling morning game drive
  • Comprehensive of government taxes, entry fees & other local levies
  • Professional English-speaking guide
Inclusions
  • Transport in a comfortable safari vehicle with a pop-up roof for optimal game viewing.
  • Nairobi National Park entry fees.
  • Pickup and drop-off within Nairobi city limits.
  • Bottled water during the tour.

United Arab Emirates

Dubai

Discover the luxury and charm of Dubai with city tours, desert safaris, shopping, and incredible nightlife.

Package Inclusions

Trip Itinerary (Experience Highlights)
  • City Tour: Explore Dubai’s most iconic attractions
  • Desert Safari: Enjoy an evening of thrills and culture with dune bashing, camel rides, henna painting, and a BBQ dinner under the stars
  • Marina Dhow Cruise: Sail through the glittering Dubai Marina aboard a traditional dhow boat, complete with dinner, music, and entertainment.
  • Discover world-class shopping at Dubai Mall or explore hidden treasures in traditional markets.
  • Nightlife & Leisure: Experience Dubai’s vibrant nightlife, from rooftop lounges to serene evening walks along the beach.
Inclusions
  • Round-trip economy class air tickets.
  • Visa processing (for Kenyan nationals).
  • Return airport transfers.
  • Desert Safari with BBQ dinner.
  • Marina Dhow Cruise with dinner.
  • Full-day Dubai City Tour.
  • Tourism Dirham tax and travel insurance included.

Tanzania

Zanzibar

Relax on pristine beaches, explore Stone Town, and immerse yourself in the vibrant Swahili culture of Zanzibar.

Package Inclusions

Trip itinerary (Experience Highlights)
  • Stroll through Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage site rich with history and charm
  • Sail across crystal-clear waters on a Safari Blue dhow experience
  • Dive into the vibrant local markets and taste authentic Zanzibari cuisine
  • Enjoy relaxing beach sunsets along the Indian Ocean
  • Take a dip in the turquoise waters of Kikuletwa Hot Springs
  • Hike to the scenic Marangu Waterfalls at the base of Mt. Kilimanjaro
  • Experience the local life and culture on Moshi and Arusha city tours
Inclusions
  • Transport in a comfortable tour bus
  • Moshi and Arusha City Tours
  • Kikuletwa Hot Springs entry
  • Marangu Waterfalls Excursion
  • Accommodation and meals on a half board basis (breakfast & dinner)

Kenya

Amboseli National Park

Experience the charm of Amboseli, where vast plains and acacia-dotted landscapes host majestic elephants and diverse wildlife, all set against the iconic snow-capped peak of Mount Kilimanjaro.

Package Inclusions

Day 1 itinerary - Nairobi to Amboseli National Park
  • Depart Nairobi at 7:00am to Amboseli National Park
  • Arrival in time for lunch
  • Relax in the afternoon (you can swim or sleep in your room)
  • Evening game drive at four
  • Dinner and overnight stay at the lodge
Day 2 itinerary
  • Early morning game drive
  • Return to the lodge for breakfast
  • After breakfast, check out and head back to Nairobi to arrive late in the afternoon
Inclusions
  • Ground transport in a comfortable safari vehicle for game viewing, photography and touring
  • Meals on full board basis (breakfast, lunch & dinner)
  • 1 night's Accommodation
  • Services of an English speaking professional driver/guide
  • Bottled water whilst on safari.
  • Begins and ends in Nairobi

Kenya

Mombasa

Hop aboard the SGR for a seamless ride to Kenya’s coastal gem. In Mombasa, unwind on sun-drenched beaches, stroll through the historic Old Town, and immerse yourself in the lively Swahili culture.

Package Inclusions

Day 1 itinerary
  • Depart Nairobi aboard the SGR train for a scenic ride to Mombasa.
  • Upon arrival, enjoy a private transfer from the Mombasa Terminus to your hotel.
  • Check in, relax, and spend the afternoon soaking up the beach vibes or taking a dip in the pool.
  • Dinner and overnight stay at your coastal hotel.
Day 2 itinerary
  • After breakfast, embark on a full-day Mombasa city tour.
  • Visit Fort Jesus, Old Town, and the Tusks on Moi Avenue, learning about the city’s deep historical roots.
  • Enjoy lunch at a local Swahili restaurant, then unwind at the beach.
  • Return to the hotel in the evening for dinner and relaxation.
Day 3 Itinerary
  • After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely morning at the beach or pool.
  • Transfer to the SGR Terminus for your return trip to Nairobi, arriving in the evening.
Inclusions
  • SGR transfers (Nairobi to Mombasa, Mombasa to Nairobi)
  • Transfer from Mombasa Terminus to hotel and back.
  • Full-day Mombasa city tour and beach activities.
  • 2 nights accommodation in a coastal hotel
  • Services of a professional English-speaking guide.
  • Bottled water during excursions.
